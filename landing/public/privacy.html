Formerly

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Formerly browser extension and related services. By using Formerly, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

In order to provide our core functionality of generating Google Forms via AI, Formerly accesses and processes the following information:

  • Google Account Information: To create forms directly in your Google Drive, we require authentication via your Google Account. We only request the minimum permissions necessary (the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/forms.body scope) to create and edit forms on your behalf.
  • User Prompts: We collect the text prompts you enter into the extension to generate a form.

2. How We Use and Share Information

We use your information strictly to provide the extension's stated functionality:

  • Form Generation: The prompts you submit are securely transmitted to our backend and processed via the OpenAI API to generate the structure of the form.
  • Form Creation: Once generated, the required structure is sent via the Google Forms API using your OAuth token to build the physical form in your Google Drive.
  • No Data Selling: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or data.
  • No Tracking: We do not use third-party behavioral tracking or build profiles on our users.

Use of Information Received from Google APIs: Formerly's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. Data Processing & Storage

  • Prompts: Your prompts are processed transiently. We do not permanently store your prompts or the generated AI responses on our servers.
  • Tokens: Your Google OAuth tokens are handled natively by your browser's extension APIs (e.g. chrome.identity) and are not stored on our external servers.
  • Forms: The forms generated by Formerly reside entirely within your own Google account. We have no access to the forms once they are created, nor to any responses submitted to those forms by other users.

4. Third-Party Services

We utilize the following third-party services to power Formerly:

  • OpenAI: Provides the language models used to interpret your prompts. Your prompts are sent to OpenAI but are not used by OpenAI to train their foundational models (per their API data usage policies).
  • Google Workspace APIs: Used to authenticate your account and programmatically build the forms.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You can revoke Formerly's access to your Google account at any time. Simply navigate to your Google Account Security settings, find "Third-party apps with account access," and remove Formerly.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at hello@formerly.com.
